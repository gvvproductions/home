GVV Productions is a non-profit film company located in The Netherlands, that makes own film projects by camera, series, and Animatons/cartoons. The projects that it makes, want to show it worldwide, and to get big audiences.

Guy van Volen (The foumder)
Born and grown up in Amsterdam at date December 21st in 1998.  starts drawing when he was a toddler, and when he got talents in drawing, he was discoverd by a Disney artist "Ruben Procopio". When he was 18 y.o, he starts making animations, cames by tutorials. and with the start of creating the sitcom of Tony Motors, He starts the non-profit film company, under the name The GVV Productions. When he was almost 20 y.o, with his new video camera with filming stuff, he starts making film projects by camera (next to animations). and now, he will try to expand this non-profit film company large in small easy steps.
